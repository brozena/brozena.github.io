### THERE ARE NO ANOMALIES IN DEEP TIME

Reconciling accentuated energy with the dirge of delusion -- 

The urgent happenstance of opportunity, the patient calculation and acceptance of risk. The slow accumulation of wise, timely, deliberate decisions, and how quickly they can unravel without vigilance over madness. One cant spend energy -- life -- all at once.

"Don't go faster than time allows. Don't outrun your shadow."

Claude Shannon said that Information is Dense if it produces a level of surprise. Complexity requires integration at different levels of scale. The intelligence of humor. Serendipity which is a strong intuition for pattern, piecing together these adjacent possibilities. The synthesis happens slowly and then all at once. All of a sudden, kairos.

The gap between sanity and insanity is [separated by time](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11097-018-9564-0). This separation tends to dissolve -- slowly, quickly, perennially, cyclically -- towards madness.

My return home to Pennsylvania, the third part of this three part [monomyth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hero%27s_journey) was temporal, geographic, and psychological. And most of all, [a choice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hero%27s_journey#Return).

*Written July, 2020 after a poor night's sleep*
